# START — Somalia flood

## Summary
Start Network funds Somalia flood response through **Start Fund Anticipation**, the
Start Fund's cross-hazard, forecast-triggered alert mechanism — not a standalone,
written EAP/AA plan the way IFRC or WFP publish for their Somalia frameworks. Any
member agency can raise an alert against a forecast; an Allocation Committee decides
whether to activate, and a Selection Committee then picks the implementing proposal(s).
The one confirmed anticipatory (pre-impact) Somalia flood activation is alert 803 in
April 2024, implemented by ActionAid UK with the Centre for Peace and Democracy; the
Anticipation Hub's 2024 listing additionally credits Muslim Aid and Action Aid
Somaliland, though their specific roles are not visible in public records.

## Trigger
No fixed indicator/threshold is publicly documented, unlike GloFAS- or SWALIM-based
EAPs. The general Start Fund Anticipation mechanism runs on member-raised alerts
assessed case-by-case against forecast/risk information, decided by an Allocation
Committee (typically within ~24 hours of an alert being raised) rather than a
pre-agreed automatic trigger. Alert 803 was raised 16 April 2024, 13:00, ahead of that
year's Gu-season river-flood risk (Juba/Shabelle basins), and approved unanimously on
17 April 2024, 15:00. The alert note itself — which would contain the specific
forecast/indicator cited — is restricted to logged-in Start Network members and could
not be read.

## Funding & scope
Alert 803: £400,000 recommended, £299,621 (≈US$374,000 at ~1.25 GBP:USD, April 2024)
ultimately allocated, following a 19 April 2024 selection-committee review of three
competing proposals; ActionAid UK (in consortium with the Centre for Peace and
Democracy) was selected as implementer. A learning meeting was held 25 July 2024.
Target population and locations for alert 803 are not stated in public sources.
The frontmatter's `prearranged_funding_usd` (US$957,854) and `target_people` (31,901)
come from the Hub's global-map inventory record for the whole 2024 Somalia-flood
listing (which names ActionAid, Muslim Aid and Action Aid Somaliland together) and
could not be reconciled against the single confirmed alert-803 tranche above — see
`extra.funding_note`.

## Activations
- **17 April 2024** — Alert 803 ("Somalia — Anticipation of flooding"), raised 16 Apr
  2024 ahead of Gu-season Juba/Shabelle flood risk; approved unanimously by the
  Allocation Committee. ActionAid UK (with the Centre for Peace and Democracy) funded
  as implementer from three competing proposals. This is the only confirmed
  *anticipatory* Somalia flood activation under Start Fund.
- Not anticipatory, so not counted above: alert 088 (reactive Somalia flood alert, date
  not public) and an October 2023 double alert shared with Nigeria (£200,000; Relief
  International + Christian Aid; ~29,950 people) — both were raised after flooding had
  already occurred.
