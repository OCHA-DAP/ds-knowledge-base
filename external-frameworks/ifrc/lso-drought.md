# IFRC — Lesotho drought

## Summary
The Lesotho Red Cross Society's (LRCS) drought Early Action Protocol (EAP2022LS01,
operation MDRLS006), approved through IFRC's Disaster Response Emergency Fund (DREF) in
December 2022 and valid through 2027. It is a two-stage design: an early, forecast-based
trigger that releases readiness funding and early-warning messaging, and a second,
confirmatory trigger that releases a further early-action funding tranche — targeting
around 20,560 people (~5,140 households) across five drought-prone districts in the
southern lowlands and Senqu river valley.

## Trigger
**First trigger** (forecast-based): a seasonal rainfall outlook — issued 19 October 2023
ahead of the 2023/24 season — forecasting below-average October-November-December (OND)
rainfall releases DREF readiness and pre-positioning funds and triggers dissemination of
early-warning messages. **Second trigger** (confirmatory, automatic): a subsequent
Lesotho Meteorological Services (LMS) report confirming that OND rainfall actually came
in below average releases a further CHF 275,738 early-action tranche. The public
documents found describe both triggers only qualitatively ("below-average OND rainfall
forecast/confirmed"); no numeric threshold or explicit lead time in days is stated.

## Funding & scope
CHF 469,480 pre-arranged via DREF (~US$549,991 per IFRC GO, fully funded): CHF 189,621
for readiness, CHF 4,121 for pre-positioning, and CHF 275,738 held for automatic release
on the second trigger. Targets ~20,560 people (~5,140 households) in Mafeteng, Mohale's
Hoek, Quthing, Qacha's Nek and Thaba-Tseka districts.

## Activations
- **19 Oct 2023 — first trigger.** A seasonal outlook forecast below-average OND
  rainfall; LRCS released readiness/pre-positioning funds and disseminated early-warning
  messages to over 10,000 people across the five target districts. The confirmatory
  second trigger (LMS report expected ~January 2024) was **not met** — OND rainfall came
  in above normal — so the CHF 275,738 early-action tranche was not released.

No further activations of this EAP are documented in the public reporting found (the
2022 and 2024/25 seasons listed on the Hub map have no corresponding activation report).
