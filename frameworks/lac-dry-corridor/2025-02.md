# Central America Dry Corridor drought — 2025-02 (El Salvador / Guatemala / Honduras)

## Summary
One umbrella framework over three countries (SLV/GTM/HND) in the Central American Dry Corridor, keyed on seasonal precipitation forecasts for two cropping seasons (Primera, Postrera). Each country activates independently when forecast seasonal rainfall falls at/below its 1-in-4-year threshold. CERF $10.5M pre-arranged for 2024–2025 (HND $4M / GTM $4M / SLV $2.5M). Updated Feb 2025; moved national → sub-national.

## Method
Monthly, the seasonal precipitation forecast (national forecast where available — Guatemala's INSIVUMEH — otherwise ECMWF SEAS5) is compared, per country/window, against an empirically-calibrated 1-in-4-year (driest-quartile) threshold over the 1981–2022 baseline. Countries run parallel-but-separate streams (scattered activation allowed). R/`targets` pipeline; monthly two-run protocol (preliminary on the 6th + final once INSIVUMEH data arrives).

## Trigger logic
- **Keys off:** seasonal accumulated precipitation forecast (mm) over the window's months.
- **Decision rule:** `forecast_mm <= empirical_RP4_threshold_mm` → activation, per country/window.
- **Calibration:** 1-in-4-year empirical RP (linear-interpolated curve, `approxfun`) per country/window/issue-month, 1981–2022 baseline.
- **Authoritative source:** regional 2025 PDF + three country annexes.

## Trigger windows

| window | season | monitored over | indicator | threshold |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A — Primera | MJJA | Mar–May | seasonal precip forecast | ≤ 1-in-4-yr (driest 25%) |
| B — Postrera | SON | Jun–Aug | seasonal precip forecast | ≤ 1-in-4-yr (driest 25%) |

## Per-country variants
One shared design (dual-window, RP-4); per-country calibration, source, and budget differ:

| country | AOI | forecast source | threshold | budget |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| El Salvador (SLV) | San Vicente dept | ECMWF SEAS5 | per-AOI RP-4 mm | $2.5M |
| Guatemala (GTM) | Chiquimula dept | **INSIVUMEH national** (overrides SEAS5) | per-AOI RP-4 mm | $4M |
| Honduras (HND) | El Paraíso + Francisco Morazán | ECMWF SEAS5 (national where possible) | per-AOI RP-4 mm | $4M |

*Implementation scope > framework scope:* the repo also computes/monitors **Nicaragua**, which is not in the official framework.

## Sources & repo completeness
- **Trigger taken from:** regional + country-annex 2025 PDFs. The richest trigger-mechanism description is an external HDX **CADC technical note v5 2025** (supporting; direct link unconfirmed).
- **Repo completeness:** **partial** — the decision rule and thresholds are present and runnable, but the repo carries Nicaragua (out of framework scope), keeps two parallel code generations (2024 national vs 2025 sub-national), and the formal trigger-mechanism report lives outside the repo.
- **Discrepancies:** NIC scope mismatch; "ECMWF" (doc) vs SEAS5 (repo); national-vs-global precedence inconsistency between umbrella and SLV annex.

## Monitoring
Email monitoring pipeline (blastula HTML via SMTP, GHA-orchestrated, semi-manual monthly). Guatemala status injected manually pending INSIVUMEH data. No dashboard app.

## Key decisions & rationale
Seasonal **precipitation forecast** chosen as the basis (earlier IRI-forecast and VHI/vegetation approaches dropped). Universal 1-in-4-yr RP set by CERF. Two windows tied to the two cropping seasons. 2025 refinement moved national → sub-national AOIs. Per-country parallel streams explicitly allow scattered activation. Guatemala's national-forecast dependency drives the two-run monthly protocol.
