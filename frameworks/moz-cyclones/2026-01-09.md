# Mozambique cyclones — 2026-01-09

## Summary
Tropical-cyclone AA across all 8 coastal provinces, keyed on RSMC La Réunion forecasts of a ≥119 km/h cyclone whose exposed area overlaps a coastal province, with an observational landfall-rainfall path. Three windows (Readiness / Action / Observational). Approved RC/HC 14 Jan 2026, ERC 24 Jan 2026; CERF ~$4.5M pre-arranged for two seasons.

## Method
RSMC La Réunion 6-hourly forecasts → wind buffers + uncertainty cone truncated at the 119 km/h contour → "exposed area"; if it overlaps a coastal province the wind trigger is met. IMERG gives the observational 3-day landfall rainfall. Monitoring labor is split: **INAM/PRISM** owns Action; **OCHA/CHD** owns Readiness + Observational.

## Trigger logic
- **Keys off:** forecast TC windspeed exposed-area over coastal provinces; observational landfall rainfall.
- **Decision rule:** exposed area of a forecast ≥119 km/h TC overlaps ≥1 of 8 coastal provinces (wind), OR 3-day province rainfall > 120 mm (obs). A rain-only activation can be STOPPED by HC/CERF within 48 h of landfall.
- **Calibration:** overall RP 4.2 yr (~1-in-3); single 119 km/h threshold (simplified from a prior two-threshold design).
- **Authoritative source:** 2026 revision PDF. The repos lag it (see discrepancies).
- **Operated by:** Action trigger runs in **INAM's PRISM** (external); the OCHA monitoring repo only operationalizes Readiness + Observational.

## Trigger windows

| window | basis | indicator | threshold | lead time | return period | monitored by |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Readiness | forecast | windspeed exposed-area | ≥ 119 km/h over a coastal province | 120–96 h | 3.0 yr | OCHA/CHD |
| Action | forecast | windspeed exposed-area | ≥ 119 km/h over a coastal province | 96–6 h | 4.2 yr | INAM/PRISM |
| Observational (wind) | forecast/obs | windspeed at landfall | ≥ 119 km/h | ~0 h | 4.2 yr | OCHA/CHD |
| Observational (rain) | observational | 3-day province rain (IMERG) | > 120 mm | day ±1 of landfall | 5.2 yr | OCHA/CHD |

(Readiness → readiness funding only; Action OR Observational → full CERF envelope.)

## Key decisions & rationale
2026 revision simplified to a single action threshold, expanded to all 8 coastal provinces, and releases the full CERF envelope on either Action or Observational. Exposed-area method (cone truncated at the 119 km/h contour + buffers) standardized. Observational rainfall "stop" lets HC/CERF halt a rain-only activation of insufficient impact.
