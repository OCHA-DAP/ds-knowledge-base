# Haiti hurricanes — 2024-08-23 (Pilote, Version #1)

## Summary
National tropical-cyclone AA pilot keyed on **both** wind speed AND 2-day national-mean rainfall while a storm is within 230 km of Haiti. Three stages (Mobilisation / Anticipatory Action / Early Response) with distinct thresholds and lead times. CERF up to $4M + co-financing $2.5M. Validated RC/HC 18 Aug 2024, ERC 23 Aug 2024.

## Method
NHC forecasts/observed tracks give wind + proximity; CHIRPS-GEFS (forecast) and IMERG (observed) give 2-day national-mean rainfall. Wind AND rain must both cross within the stage's lead-time window. Monitoring + certification by OCHA/CHD (pipeline folded into this repo).

## Trigger logic
- **Keys off:** max sustained wind (kt) AND 2-day total precipitation (national mean), gated by distance ≤230 km.
- **Decision rule:** each stage fires when wind ≥ its threshold AND rain ≥ its threshold within its lead window.
- **Calibration:** return-period against historical storms (Matthew 2016, 2008 storms, Sandy 2012); combined mechanism RP ≈ 3.0 yr (CERF "~1-in-3-year").
- **Authoritative source:** 2024 PDF (Tableau 4); code `THRESHS` matches.

## Trigger windows

| window (PDF → code) | basis | wind | 2-day rain | lead time | return period |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mobilisation → readiness | forecast | ≥ 64 kt | ≥ 42 mm | ≤ 120 h | 3.4 yr |
| Anticipatory Action → action | forecast | ≥ 64 kt | ≥ 42 mm | ≤ 72 h | 4.8 yr |
| Early Response → obsv | observational | ≥ 50 kt | ≥ 70 mm | ~+1 day | 4.0 yr |

(All gated by ≤230 km. Obs rain (70mm) > forecast rain (42mm) corrects CHIRPS-GEFS↔IMERG bias. AA implies Mobilisation since they share thresholds.)

## Sources & repo completeness
- **Trigger taken from:** `framework_doc` Tableau 4 — confirmed by `THRESHS`.
- **Repo completeness:** **full** — all three stages, both indicators, the 230 km gate and lead windows are in `constants.py` + `monitoring_utils.py`, plus the full monitoring/email pipeline.
- **Discrepancies:** PDF Tableau 1 typo (70/42 swapped) vs authoritative Tableau 4; stage relabeling (Mobilisation→readiness etc.) undocumented; active post-v1 R&D in `exploration/` diverging from published v1.

## Monitoring
Event-driven monitoring folded into this repo — see [pipelines/hti-hurricanes-monitoring](../../pipelines/hti-hurricanes-monitoring.md). Forecast check dispatched by `ds-nhc-forecast` on each new track; obsv check by the IMERG pipeline; CHIRPS-GEFS updated daily (cron 08:50 UTC).

## Key decisions & rationale
Wind AND rain (not wind alone) to catch wet/slow storms. Added observational post-landfall trigger for rapid intensification / late track shifts missed by forecasts. Higher obs rain threshold corrects forecast↔observation bias. ~3-yr global RP for CERF eligibility.
