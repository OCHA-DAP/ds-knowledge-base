# Burkina Faso drought — 2026-04-17

## Summary
Seasonal drought AA over two regions (Liptako, Yaadga — 4 provinces), keyed on an April **SEAS5** seasonal-precipitation tercile forecast plus an August/September **ASAP** biomass observational trigger. Two independent, non-cumulative triggers; single 100% CERF disbursement (up to $8M) on whichever fires first. Approved RC 7 Apr 2026, ERC 17 Apr 2026; valid 2026–2027.

## Method
Trigger 1: SEAS5 forecast issued in April, pixel-wise percentile rank vs 1981–2025, clipped to the AOI; activate if ≥50% of AOI area is in the lowest tercile. Trigger 2: JRC ASAP Level-3 alert in ≥2 provinces between dekads 21–26. OCHA/CHD monitors and notifies.

## Trigger logic
- **Keys off:** SEAS5 seasonal-precip tercile (forecast); ASAP biomass/water-balance alert (observational).
- **Decision rule:** Trigger 1: `frac_AOI_area_in_lowest_tercile ≥ 0.50` (SEAS5, April). Trigger 2: ASAP Level-3 in ≥2 AOI provinces, dekads 21–26. Logical OR; non-cumulative.
- **Calibration:** ~6-year combined return period; SEAS5 threshold ≈ 20th percentile (lower quintile), 1981–2025 reference.
- **Authoritative source:** 2026 PDF; repo `monitoring_2026.md` implements Trigger 1, `asap.py` Trigger 2.

## Trigger windows

| trigger | basis | indicator | threshold | window | releases |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Trigger 1 | forecast | SEAS5 seasonal precip (Jun–Oct) tercile | ≥ 50% of AOI in lowest tercile | issued April | 100% CERF (≤$8M) |
| Trigger 2 | observational | JRC ASAP biomass/water-balance | Level-3 alert in ≥2 provinces | dekads 21–26 (late Jul–mid Sep) | 100% CERF (≤$8M) |

(Independent, non-cumulative — first to fire disburses everything.)

## Monitoring
OCHA/CHD monitoring via `analysis/monitoring_2026.md` (notebook, not packaged). No deployed app.

## Key decisions & rationale
Switched forecast source IRI NMME → **ECMWF SEAS5** (SEAS5 tercile percentile as the closest analogue to the original IRI tercile). Added an independent ASAP biomass trigger so activation can occur intra-season if the forecast misses. 2026 tightened targeting to the two highest-vulnerability regions and raised the forecast area threshold to 50% (~6-yr combined RP).
