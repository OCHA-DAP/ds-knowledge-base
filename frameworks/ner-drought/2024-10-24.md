# Niger drought — 2024-10-24

## Summary
National drought AA framework keyed on seasonal rainfall forecasts for the July–September (JAS) season over Niger south of 17°N, with an observational SPI backstop. Two intervention windows ("save the harvest" / "mitigate impact") tied to distinct activity packages; CERF up to USD 15M over two years. Update of the 2022 pilot (which activated in Aug 2022).

## Method
IRI seasonal forecast probabilities of below-average JAS rainfall (OCHA Certification model, 35% frequency) are averaged over the south-of-17°N zone and compared against the historical forecast distribution. An observational June–July SPI (ENACTS) provides a second, later path. Activation auto-releases CERF for the corresponding package.

## Trigger logic
- **Keys off:** IRI forecast prob of below-average JAS rainfall (forecast); June–July SPI (observational).
- **Decision rule:** within a window, two *consecutive* monthly forecasts must both land in the driest 35% (AND); across pairs and across forecast-vs-SPI, OR.
- **Calibration:** combined design return period ≈ 3.5 years; two consecutive forecasts required for persistence (false-alarm suppression).
- **Authoritative source:** 2024 framework PDF (Maproom 2 model). The repo is an exploratory re-derivation/explorer.
- **Operated by:** the live trigger is **IRI Maproom 2**, operated with WFP/AGRHYMET/DMN/DNPGCA — *not* this repo.

## Trigger windows

| window | basis | indicator | threshold | months (consecutive pairs) | return period | releases |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Window 1 "save the harvest" | forecast | IRI below-avg JAS | driest 35% | Jan+Feb or Feb+Mar | — | Window 1 package |
| Window 2 (forecast path) | forecast | IRI below-avg JAS | driest 35% | Mar+Apr, Apr+May, or May+Jun | — | Window 2 package |
| Window 2 (obs path) | observational | Jun–Jul SPI (ENACTS) | lowest 35% | reported Aug | — | Window 2 package |

(Combined target RP ≈ 3.5 yr. Within a pair = AND; across pairs / forecast-vs-SPI = OR.)

## Sources & repo completeness
- **Trigger taken from:** `framework_doc` (2024 PDF).
- **Repo completeness:** **partial** — reproduces the forecast component (rolling-window percentile, consecutive-pair logic) with a calibration explorer app, but does not implement the two-window/dual-package structure and its default obs threshold diverges from the PDF.
- **Discrepancies:** (1) obs threshold 15% (repo default) vs 35% (PDF); (2) windows collapsed into one combined trigger; (3) undocumented rolling-vs-expanding reference window choice; (4) SPI column labelled "Aug" (Jun–Jul rainfall, Aug reporting point).

## Monitoring
IRI updates the Maproom mid-month Jan–Aug and reports trigger status to OCHA. No OCHA-run monitoring pipeline in this repo (the operational surface is IRI's).

## Key decisions & rationale
35% threshold flags seasons projected worse than ~65% of history, balanced to a ~3.5-yr RP. Dual basis (forecast + SPI backstop) so a missed forecast signal can still be caught by observed deficit. Two windows align to the agricultural crisis timeline. Thresholds locked to real historical observations (no interpolation) in the repo for interpretability.
