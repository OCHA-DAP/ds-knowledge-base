First activation of the endorsed framework. On 8 September 2025, GloFAS and Google Flood Hub forecasts exceeded the endorsed riverine thresholds for the Benue River at Wuroboki (Adamawa). CERF USD 5M + NHF USD 2M (USD 7M total pre-arranged envelope) released; within ~72 hours WFP delivered anticipatory multi-purpose cash to over 83,500 people across four Adamawa LGAs, ~350,000 people reached across the 7 target LGAs.
