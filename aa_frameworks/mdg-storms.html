Cyclone Gezani — Madagascar's FIRST anticipatory action for cyclones. Framework triggered 9 Feb 2026 when Météo-Madagascar forecasts met the Scenario 3 threshold (intense TC, ≥166 km/h, within 100 km of coast). BOTH windows fired — the readiness trigger (72–120 h) followed by the action trigger (≤72 h) — per the CHD team (@t-downing). The FULL US$3M pre-arranged CERF envelope was released ~1 day ahead of landfall to six UN agencies (IOM, UNICEF, UNFPA, WHO, WFP, FAO), targeting >93,000 vulnerable people. Gezani made landfall near Toamasina on 10 Feb 2026 as an intense TC (winds up to 250 km/h). Confirmed via OCHA Gezani flash updates (Feb 2026) + the Anticipation Hub.
