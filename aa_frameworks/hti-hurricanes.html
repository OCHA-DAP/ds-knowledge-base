Activated for Hurricane Melissa (Category 5). On 21 October 2025 forecasts showed Melissa would be near Haiti within ~72h; within one hour the pre-agreed USD 4M CERF envelope was released to WFP, UNICEF and IOM to support >100,000 people in Haiti's high-risk southern departments. The trigger threshold prompted government-led early-warning messaging to ~3.5M people and cash transfers of ~USD 0.9M to ~50,000 people. Fired under the 2024-08-23 endorsed (v1) version.
