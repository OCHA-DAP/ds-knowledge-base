First-ever activation of the Nepal AA pilot. Readiness trigger reached 2 Oct 2022 (Kailali/Bardiya/Banke flood warnings); action trigger reached 7 Oct 2022; river crossed the danger level at Chisapani 9 Oct 2022. CERF authorized $3.2M for UNFPA, UN Women and WFP to deliver cash, relief kits and health/GBV services ahead of peak flooding in Kailali, Bardiya and Banke districts. Also recorded on the 2025-08-25 page as a lessons-learned reference.
2021-08-13readiness (Koshi/Chatara, east) partialannouncement ↗21-RR-NPL-48553 ↗$3.2M00
Readiness-only FALSE ALARM, first-ever trigger of the pilot. GloFAS showed a 73% chance of a 1-in-2-year flood at Chatara on 18 Aug; CERF initiated disbursement of $3.2M across the 5 pre-approved east-Nepal projects (application 21-RR-NPL-48553). The action trigger was never reached before the government declared the monsoon over (11 Oct); agencies distributed nothing, refunded 100% of action funds plus unused readiness funds, and zero beneficiaries were reached — a scenario envisaged in the pilot design. The west readiness trigger never fired.
