SSD (South Sudan). CERF released US$15M anticipatorily ahead of the 2022 rainy-season floods, complemented by US$4M from the South Sudan Humanitarian Fund (SSHF). Focused on the Bentiu IDP camp and surrounding areas, Unity State (>320,000 at high risk). Reached 301,649 people across WASH, Nutrition, Agriculture, Camp Management, Shelter, Health and Protection. Not fired by a formal/quantitative trigger — released on risk analysis and forecasts (judgement-based AA). Funds used to construct/reinforce 55+ km of dykes, reportedly preventing ~100,000 people from being evacuated.
